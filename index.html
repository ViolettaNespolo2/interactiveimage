Times Square in Numbers

Energy consumption in the Theater District has been said to be 161 Megawhats, according to ConEdison.

This same energy could be used to light up an avereage of 161,000 homes in the US.

According to Business Insider, in 2017, there are 55 giant LED screens in the square.

The cost of running Times Square everyday is estimated to be around $25,000, according to Business Insider.

According to Times Square NYC - who manages the square - daily visitors on average were 390,368 in 2018.

This is roughly translated into 1.5 million daily impressions of the signage in the square by Investopedia.com - not counting New Year's Even ball drop, apperances in movies, TVs or other.

Cost of advertising in Times Square is around 1.7 cents per impression - compared to an average of 0.2 to 0.5 on other billboards in USA.

This totals around $1 to $4 million a year to advertise in the square. Nonetheless, the price can get up to $3 million a month to advertise on the largest billboards states Investopedia.com.
